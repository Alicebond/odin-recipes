Steak

Description

I had a steak with this marinade, and it was the best steak I ever had.

Ingredient

  • 1 pinch seasoned salt
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon, crushed
  • Â½ cup butter, melted

Step

  1. Stir the seasoning salt, garlic powder, Greek seasoning, and bouillon cube into the melted butter. Mix until the bouillon cube dissolves.
  2. Pour the marinade into a wide and shallow dish and place a steak of your choice into the marinade, turning to coat both sides.
  3. Cook or grill as desired.
